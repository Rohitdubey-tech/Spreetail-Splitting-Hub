## 3. Ingestion Rules Applied

* **Exchange Rate Conversion**: Foreign currency amounts (USD) are converted to INR at a fixed rate of `83.0`.
* **Settlement Mapping**: Transactions describing direct payments between flatmates (with no split details) are logged in the `Settlement` table rather than creating dummy group expenses.
* **Precision Rounding**: Decimal amounts with high precision are rounded to two decimal places.
* **Percentage Normalization**: Proportional adjustment is applied to percentage splits that do not sum to exactly 100%.
